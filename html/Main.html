Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Driven by Passion

Coffee Roots was born from a simple belief: exceptional coffee starts long before the roast. We partner directly with small-scale farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond, building relationships that are fair, transparent, and lasting.

Every bag we sell represents months of careful cultivation, careful selection, and careful roasting. We do not cut corners because your cup deserves the best.
